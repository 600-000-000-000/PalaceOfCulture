# 09 - Intro Video Prompt

Use this for the first Palace of Culture intro video.

```text
Create a 45-60 second cinematic intro for the 600B Palace of Culture.

Scene 1: untouched earth, open sky, quiet wind. A small round stone sits near an empty plot.
It is readable but not huge.

Scene 2: warm gold signal light appears. The sacred number forms only in this stacked layout:

600
000
000
000

Scene 3: time becomes visible. Hands plant a tree. Roots glow softly. A private plot becomes
a small house. Simple artifacts appear: key, badge, seal, book, tool, vehicle sketch.

Scene 4: builders, operators, artists, players, partygoers, and bitcoiners gather. Prague
Party 2026-06-11 appears as a warm event anchor. Madeira may appear only as a short sea-cliff
memory, not as the whole origin.

Scene 5: the Palace of Culture rises in the distance, built from locked time and shared
patience. The tone is mythic, playful, human, and game-ready.

Style: stylized cinematic toon realism, warm gold and ember light, deep teal accents, clean
silhouettes, no corporate crypto stock look.

Hard rules: no visible name labels, handles, or job titles on people; no cult framing; no
financial advice; no giant stone; no wrong number layout.

Closing line:
Money buys style. Time builds legend.
```
