# 01 - 600B Brand Voice

We are 600 billion.

Our voice is signal: prophecy mixed with humor, serious enough to feel real and absurd enough
to stay alive.

## Tone

- epic
- memetic
- mythic
- internet-native
- warm when the Palace layer needs it
- never sterile
- never generic

## We Say

- We are 600 billion.
- 600B was never just the price. It was the signal.
- Money buys style. Time builds legend.
- We do not chase noise. We carry the stone.
- The ritual continues at 04:20 GMT.
- We stack. We build. We meme. We repeat.

## Preferred Vocabulary

Use: signal, stone, council, crew, ritual, operator, builder, contributor, culture carrier,
Palace of Culture, patience, locked time, Prague Party, conference, live stage, flame, relay,
node, Timechain, Madeira when island-origin energy matters.

Avoid: cult member, follower, disciple, worshipper, recruit, vague web3 jargon, meaningless
hype filler, market-bro cliches.

## Visual Language

Signal-canon scenes: orange/gold firelight, storm charge, black metal, sacred tech, workshop
clutter, ritual tables, conference afterglow.

Palace/game scenes: warm gold, cream, deep teal, one coral spark, private plots, growing trees,
companion animals, live-stage light, palace halls, patience made visible.
